Welcome To Aviation School

Aviation School was established in the year 1860 in Kolkata, India by the Jesuits (missionary priests belonging to the order of the Society of Jesus). The School comprises of two school buildings separated by an intersection; one building pertains to the Primary Section (classes Prep - 5) and the other houses the Secondary section (classes 6-12). The Secondary section shares the campus alongside the College. The school is a Catholic Institution but admits and houses students from all religious denominations, caste and creed. In fact non-Christian students make up a large segment of the school.

Academic Integration

Aviation School as it stands today is affiliated to the Council for the Indian School Certificate Examinations that conducts ICSE and ISC Examinations for class 10 and 12 respectively. The school is also affiliated to the West Bengal Board of Secondary Education that conducts the Madhyamik Examination for class 10. Students may choose between the two boards as per their choice and preference. But in the recent years there has not been too many registrations for the Madhyamik Examination. Most students or all are opting for the Indian School Certificate Examinations in Class 10.

Academic Session

The academic session of the school is from April to March. Saturday and Sunday are the normal weekly holidays.

Provide Best Facilities To Your Children

Library

The school library,equipped with over 5000 titles,is a heaven where our students can relax with a book,read newspapers and magazines,attend story telling sessions and even watch documentation and movies.
Computer Room

The computer laboratory is a hub for e-learning where students from class 2 upwards have access to computers and are trained in programming and networking to empower them to take their place as responsible citizen in the digital world.
Chemisty Lab

The school has a well-equipped chemistry laboratory for the middle and senior school.Our students learn through experimentation and innovation.
Physics Lab

The school has a well-equipped physics laboratory with all the equipment necessary to ensure our students have a hands on knowledge of physics.
Biology Lab

The school is equipped with a state of the art biology laboratory.It is spacious and can easily accommodate forty students at a time.The human skeletal model is a key to learning about our anatomy and our lab has an excellent and accurate skeletal model.
Playground

The shcool has the luxury of a large playground where our students enjoy the freedom to run and play or simply relax in the shade of a mango tree.The school field is also used for senior,junior and primary Annual Sports Days.
Music Training

The school orchestra comprising students from class III upwards performs at all major school events such as Prize Day and Founder's Day.The Students are trained in playing instuments such as Violin and Cello.
Nursery Play Area

The school has a Shady Nook-the Nursery Play Area where the littlest Martinians can play together in a safe and happy environment.
